About author

The author has a Master's degree in Cultural Studies and previously worked as a marketing specialist in a cultural institution. However, an interest in website management led her to take a graphics course and secure an internship at an advertising agency. She then completed a postgraduate course in front-end web application development and successfully developed a commercial website using React. This experience helped her secure a position at Tom&Co, a Magento e-commerce agency. There she worked for 5 months as a junior front-end developer, gaining experience in Angular, TypeScript, Magento and Git workflow. As a member of the SCRUM team, she was responsible for the maintenance and development of e-commerce websites. She is currently enrolled in the Frontend course at RS School to further develop her skills.

RS School Angular course

RS School is an online education platform that offers a range of courses for developers to improve their skills and learn new technologies. RS School courses cover various topics such as front-end web development, back-end web development, full-stack web development, mobile development and more. Courses are available in English and Russian and are taught by experienced industry instructors. One of the unique features of RS School's courses is that they are project-based, meaning that students learn by working on real-world projects and building practical applications. In addition, RS School provides students with access to mentors who can offer guidance and support throughout the course. RS School is an excellent resource for developers looking to improve their skills and gain practical experience in a collaborative learning environment.
